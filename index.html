🏆 Featured Project
Ace — AI Voice Agent for Sports & Events

A fully production-deployed AI voice agent built for World of Sports Fun — a multi-activity venue offering bowling, laser tag, and private event packages. Ace handles inbound calls 24/7 with natural conversation: answering business questions, providing instant price quotes, checking availability, capturing event inquiries and leads, booking events, and even recognizing returning callers by name.

Powered by 8 live AI tools — get_business_info, capture_inquiry, capture_lead, get_price_quote, check_availability, book_event, recognize_caller, and end_of_call_report. Every booking triggers an automatic customer confirmation email and an owner notification. All calls are logged to Supabase, then synced hourly to Google Sheets (Inquiries, Leads, Call Logs tabs) and Google Calendar.

Backed by 7 automated workflows: Voice Agent, Hourly Sync (Sheets + Calendar), Manual Sync, Daily Date Updater, Web Call Token Generator, Daily Owner Summary Email, and 3-Day Event Reminder Email — all running live on Railway.
